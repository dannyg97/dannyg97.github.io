About

Hey there! My name is Danny and I'm one trimester away from earning my degree at UNSW with a Bachelor of Computer Science. My university experience with software development, as well as my extracurricular activities involving hackathons and project management, has given me a strong starting skill set for the software development industry. My passion in this field has led me to seek out learning opportunities in all facets of computer science, with a particular interest in full stack application development.
Let me tell you a few things things about myself that my resume doesn't already say!

I have a fondness for teaching that was realised through my after-school tutoring experience for BitByBit at Parramatta High School where I taught Python to junior high schoolers. During my time as their teacher, I realised that teaching passionate people on subjects they're passionate about is immensely gratifying and it's a significant aspect of software development and also what makes it so interesting.

I've also realised how important it is to be able to take criticism and mold it into a learning experience through my involvement in RSE's STEM Leaders Program, where I worked closely with Accenture as the mock project manager for a group of 4 other individuals to create a digital dashboard hosted on Splunk - a data analytics and visualisation tool - that emulates a physical electronic dashboard through image recognition using Python's OpenCV3 library, which can send API commands to the physical dashboard.

When I'm not coding or watching a concert, you'll likely catch me bouldering at Parramatta, hiking around the Blue Mountains, or doodling artworks in the comfort of my home.
